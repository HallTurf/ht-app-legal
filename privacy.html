Privacy Policy

Palper HT Invoice Sync, operated by Hall Turf, Inc. ("we", "us"). Last updated June 1, 2026. Contact: brock@hallturf.com.

1. Scope

This application is an internal tool used by Hall Turf, Inc. to connect to its own QuickBooks Online company file and automatically create invoices for completed jobs. It is not offered to or used by the public or by any third parties.

2. Data we access

Through the QuickBooks Online API, the application reads and writes accounting records belonging to Hall Turf, Inc. only — customers, estimates, and invoices — for the sole purpose of generating and sending invoices for work Hall Turf, Inc. has performed.

3. How data is used

Accessed data is used exclusively to create, update, and send Hall Turf, Inc. invoices. We do not sell, rent, or share QuickBooks data with any third party. No customer data is used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to invoicing.

4. Storage and security

OAuth credentials are stored locally on Hall Turf, Inc.-controlled systems with restricted file permissions and are not transmitted to any third party other than Intuit's QuickBooks API. Access tokens rotate on use.

5. Data retention and deletion

Hall Turf, Inc. may disconnect the application at any time from the QuickBooks Online "Connected Apps" settings, which revokes its access. Locally stored credentials are deleted on disconnection.

6. Changes

Updates to this policy will be posted on this page. Questions: brock@hallturf.com.
