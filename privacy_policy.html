1. Who this applies to

The "Hall Turf Inc Connector" application ("the App") is a private, internal integration used solely by Hall Turf, Inc. to connect its own QuickBooks Online company to its own back-office automation. The App is not offered to, marketed to, or used by any third party or member of the public.

2. What data the App accesses

Through the Intuit QuickBooks Online API (scope com.intuit.quickbooks.accounting), the App reads and writes accounting records belonging to Hall Turf, Inc., including invoices, customers, payments, and related transaction data. It accesses only the Hall Turf, Inc. company that the authorizing user explicitly connects.

3. How the data is used

Data is used only to automate Hall Turf, Inc.'s own bookkeeping: creating and sending invoices, recording customer payments, and reconciling accounts receivable. The App does not sell, share, rent, or disclose any data to third parties. No data is used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to Hall Turf, Inc.'s internal accounting.

4. Where the data is stored

OAuth tokens and accounting data retrieved by the App are stored locally on Hall Turf, Inc. controlled systems and its private repositories. Tokens are stored in restricted, non-public configuration. No customer accounting data is transmitted to any external party other than Intuit's own API.

5. Data retention and deletion

Accounting data is retained only as long as needed for bookkeeping and as required by applicable tax and recordkeeping law. The connection can be revoked at any time from within QuickBooks Online (Apps > Connected apps > Disconnect), after which the App ceases all access and stored tokens are invalidated.

6. Security

Access is limited to authorized Hall Turf, Inc. personnel. OAuth credentials are kept in restricted storage and are never exposed publicly. Tokens auto-rotate on use.

This policy may be updated; the effective date above reflects the latest revision.
